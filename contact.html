Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical construction project take in Juba?

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A residential house typically takes 6-12 months, while commercial projects can take 12-24 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and strive to complete projects on schedule despite local challenges.

What construction materials do you use?

We use high-quality, durable materials suitable for South Sudan's climate. We source locally where possible and import specialized materials when needed. All materials meet international standards and undergo quality checks before use.

Do you offer financing options for construction projects?

While we don't provide direct financing, we work with several financial institutions in Juba that offer construction loans. We can connect you with our trusted banking partners and help prepare the necessary documentation for loan applications.

How do you handle project delays due to weather or other factors?

We build contingency plans into all our project schedules. Our experienced team knows how to work around South Sudan's rainy season and other local challenges. We maintain open communication with clients about any delays and work efficiently to minimize their impact.

What areas in South Sudan do you serve?

While our main office is in Juba, we undertake construction projects throughout South Sudan. We have successfully completed projects in various states and are equipped to manage remote projects with proper planning and local partnerships.
